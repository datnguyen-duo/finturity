About Finturity

A first-hand,
personable approach
to retirement plans.

In the often confusing world of finance, Finturity guides clients through the maze by providing tailored retirement plan solutions. We understand every organization is unique, and therefore, we strive to become dependable collaborators. We want our clients to feel like we’re part of their team — by providing well-designed retirement plans that secure the best talent as well as the organization’s future stability.

We assess, plan, and
anticipate your organization’s
financial challenges.

01

We support

Offering a helping hand is what we’re most known for, and our personalized guidance helps organizations design better retirement plans — and we do so together.

02

We educate

We understand that deep financial knowledge is not in our clients’ toolkits. We are aware of this and offer step-by-step advice and educational content that cuts through the noise.

03

We empower

We believe financial planning empowers our country’s nonprofits. Our financial services help our clients make the most competent financial decisions.

The leadership team

Sarah E. Burton, CRPC®

Founder & Financial Advisor

Sarah Burton is a financial planner and the founder of Monument Retirement Advisors. She began in 2008 by working with her father James Gibney, who provided retirement plans to over 100 nonprofit organizations, large and small, in and around Washington, DC. Upon his retirement at the end of 2016, Sarah took over full ownership of the practice and continues to help organizations navigate the complex world of retirement plans. In 2018, Sarah re-branded the practice as Monument Retirement Advisors and expanded her services to meet the growing needs of employees and other individuals as they prepare for retirement.

Sarah has passed the Series 6, 63, and 65 FINRA exams and holds her life, health and variable annuity licenses. In 2016, she obtained her Chartered Retirement Planning CounselorSM (CRPC®) designation from the College for Financial Planning. Sarah was named Lincoln Financial Advisor's 2017 ABS Advisor of Now for the Northeast Region and the 2017 ABS Advisor of the Year, New Organization.

Sarah graduated summa cum laude from the University of Mary Washington in 2007 with a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration. She lives with her husband and two young sons in Gaithersburg, MD. She teaches Irish dance once a week, loves to read, and roots for the Nationals!
